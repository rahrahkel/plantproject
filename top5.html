Top 5 Easy Houseplants for Beginners

Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

Known for its tall, upright leaves with striking green and yellow patterns, the snake plant is nearly indestructible. It thrives on neglect, needs minimal water, and tolerates low light. It is perfect for beginners or busy people. Bonus: it also helps clean indoor air!

Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

With trailing vines and heart-shaped leaves, pothos is both beautiful and forgiving. It grows quickly, even in low light, and only needs water when the soil feels dry. Great for hanging baskets or shelves. Comes in several fun varieties like golden, marble, and neon!

ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

Glossy dark green leaves and tough-as-nails nature make the ZZ plant a favorite. It tolerates low light, infrequent watering, and dry indoor air. It‚Äôs a great option for office spaces or low-light apartments.

Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum)

Elegant white blooms and lush green leaves make the peace lily a stunning addition to any room. It prefers indirect light and moist soil‚Äî and it tells you when it‚Äôs thirsty by drooping slightly. Easy to care for and great for improving indoor air quality.
